## Design Principles Applied

1. **Decimal arithmetic** - No float precision errors in money
2. **Frozen dataclasses** - Immutable, hashable, cache-friendly
3. **CLI-first** - Designed for terminal use during market hours
4. **JSON export** - Integration with dashboards/other tools
5. **Fail fast validation** - Clear error messages, no silent failures
