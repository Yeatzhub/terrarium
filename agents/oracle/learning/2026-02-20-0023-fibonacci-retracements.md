## What Fibonacci Measures

Based on the golden ratio (1.618), Fibonacci levels identify potential support/resistance where price may reverse in a trending market.

**Retracement Levels:** 23.6%, 38.2%, 50%, 61.8%, 78.6%  
**Extension Levels:** 138.2%, 161.8%, 200%, 261.8%, 423.6%

**Why it works:** Institutional algorithms and retail traders both watch these levels. Self-fulfilling prophecy creates reaction zones.

---

## Core Use Cases

### 1. Retracement Entries — "Buy the Dip"

**Setup Requirements:**
- Clear impulse wave (strong directional move)
- Timeframe: 4H+ for reliability (15m+ for scalps)
- Volume decreasing on pullback

**Entry Rules:**

| Level | Trade Type | Probability |
|-------|------------|-------------|
| 38.2% | Shallow pullback, strong trend | 60-70% |
| 50% | Moderate pullback, balanced | 65-75% |
| 61.8% | Deep pullback, trend test | 70-80% |
| 78.6% | Extreme pullback, low confidence | 50% |

**Long Entry Framework (Bullish Trend):**
1. Identify clear impulse wave (low to high)
2. Draw Fib from swing low to swing high
3. Wait for price to enter 38.2%–61.8% zone
4. Look for bounce confirmation:
   - Bullish engulfing candle
   - Rejection wick at level
   - Volume spike on reversal
5. Entry: Break of reversal candle high
6. Stop: Below 78.6% or structure low
7. Target: Prior high or 1.618 extension

### 2. Confluence Zones — High Probability

**Stacked Confluences (Highest Probability):**
- Fib level + prior support/resistance
- Fib level + moving average (50/200 EMA)
- Fib level + pivot point
- Fib level + Order Block (SMC)
- Fib level + psychological number ($100, $50)

**Example:**
- Price pulling back in uptrend
- 50% Fib aligns with 50 EMA
- Level was prior resistance (now support)
- Daily pivot at same zone
- **Result:** Triple confluence, 80%+ win rate

### 3. Extensions — Profit Targets

Once retracement entry works, use extensions for targets.

| Extension | Target Use |
|-----------|------------|
| 1.0 | Measured move (equal to impulse) |
| 1.272 | Conservative extension |
| 1.618 | Primary target (golden ratio) |
| 2.0 | Aggressive, strong trend |
| 2.618 | Extended trend, parabolic |

**Target Stacking:**
1. Scale out 1/3 at 1.272
2. Scale out 1/3 at 1.618
3. Trail stop on final 1/3

## Fibonacci + Market Structure

### Trend Continuation Play

1. HTF trend identified (Bullish BOS series)
2. Pullback to 50-61.8% Fib on LTF
3. LTF structure holds (higher low maintained)
4. Fib level = Institutional accumulation zone
5. Entry: Reclaim of structure + Fib support
6. Stop: Below Fib + structure
7. Target: 1.618 extension

### Breakout Retest

1. Price breaks major resistance (BOS)
2. Pullback to broken level (now support)
3. Broken level aligns with 38.2-50% Fib
4. Entry: Hold of Fib + broken resistance
5. Confirmation: Bullish candle at confluence

**Rule:** Higher timeframe Fibs dominate. A weekly 61.8% overrides a 15m 38.2%.

## Practical Entry Framework

### Bullish Retracement Setup

**Step-by-Step:**
1. Identify impulse wave (strong green candles)
2. Swing low = Fib 0%
3. Swing high = Fib 100%
4. Mark 38.2%, 50%, 61.8% levels
5. Set alerts at 50% and 61.8%
6. Wait for price to reach zone
7. Look for:
   - Bullish candlestick pattern
   - Volume increase
   - Smaller timeframe BOS
8. Entry: Break of bullish candle high
9. Stop: Below 78.6% or below structure
10. Target: 1.618 extension or prior structure

### When NOT to Trade Fib

- No clear impulse wave
- Choppy, range-bound market
- Against major HTF trend
- News event imminent
- Low volume environment

## Fibonacci Clusters

Multiple Fibs aligning = cluster = major S/R.

**Example:**
- Daily Fib: 50% at $100
- 4H Fib: 61.8% at $99.80
- 1H Fib: 38.2% at $100.20
- **Cluster zone:** $99.80–100.20

Action: Treat as single high-probability zone. Stop below entire cluster.

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Why It Fails | Fix |
|---------|--------------|-----|
| Forcing Fib on every chart | Needs clear impulse | Only draw on valid swings |
| Ignoring trend | Counter-trend Fibs fail | Trade with HTF trend only |
| Entering without confirmation | Price can slice through | Wait for reversal candle |
| Wrong swing points | Invalidates entire tool | Use clear swing highs/lows |
| Holding through 78.6% break | Trend likely reversing | Stop at 78.6% + buffer |
| No confluence check | Lower probability | Stack with other S/R |
