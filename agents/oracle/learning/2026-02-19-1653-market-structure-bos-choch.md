## Core Concepts (SMC/ICT Framework)

Market structure reveals trend health. Three patterns tell you everything: **Break of Structure (BOS)**, **Change of Character (ChoCH)**, and **Market Structure Shift (MSS)**.

---

## 1. Break of Structure (BOS) — Trend Continuation

### What is BOS?
Price breaks beyond the prior swing high (bullish) or swing low (bearish), confirming trend continuation.

**Bullish BOS:**
```
Swing High 1 ───┐
                │
Pullback    ────┘
                │
Swing High 2 ───┴───→ BOS (breaks SH1)
```

**Requirements:**
- Clear swing structure exists (higher highs/lows or lower highs/lows)
- Break is decisive (close beyond, not just wick)
- Followed by displacement (strong move, not chop)

### Trading BOS

| Condition | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| BOS in HTF trend | With-trend continuation entry |
| BOS + FVG | High probability, enter in gap |
| BOS + OB | Entry at Order Block retest |
| BOS in chop | False signal, avoid |

**Entry:**
- Aggressive: Break of BOS candle
- Conservative: Pullback to broken swing level (now support/resistance)
- Optimal: BOS + return to fresh OB or FVG

---

## 2. Change of Character (ChoCH) — Internal Structure Change

### What is ChoCH?
Price breaks internal structure within a trend — hints at potential trend change OR deep pullback. **Weaker signal than MSS.**

**Bullish to Bearish ChoCH:**
```
Higher Highs ─────┐
                  │ Trend up
Higher Lows ──────┘
                  
Lower Low ────────┐ ← ChoCH (breaks last HL)
                  │ Early warning
?               ──┘ (need MSS to confirm)
```

### ChoCH vs. BOS

| | BOS | ChoCH |
|---|-----|-------|
| **Direction** | Continuation | Potential reversal |
| **Strength** | Strong | Moderate (warning) |
| **Action** | Enter with trend | Caution, reduce size |
| **Confirmation** | Immediate | Needs MSS or structure break |

**How to Trade ChoCH:**
1. **Don't reverse yet** — it's a warning, not a signal
2. Reduce position size if in trend direction
3. Tighten stops (trend may be ending)
4. Watch for MSS as confirmation
5. Can take counter-trend scalp to internal liquidity

---

## 3. Market Structure Shift (MSS) — Trend Reversal

### What is MSS?
Price breaks the **origin** of the prior trend — the swing low that started the uptrend (or swing high that started downtrend). **Confirmed trend change.**

**Bullish to Bearish MSS:**
```
Origin (Swing Low) ───┐ ← This is the key level
                      │ Uptrend
Higher Highs ─────────┤
                      │
Lower Low (breaks origin) ← MSS confirmed
```

**Requirements:**
- Must break the swing that **started** the trend (not just any swing)
- Decisive break with close beyond
- Followed by lower high (bearish confirmation structure)

### MSS = Go Time

**Bullish MSS → Bearish:**
- Exit all longs immediately
- Can enter shorts at retest of broken origin
- Target: Prior lows or opposite liquidity

**Bearish MSS → Bullish:**
- Exit all shorts immediately  
- Can enter longs at retest of broken origin
- Target: Prior highs or opposite liquidity

---

## Structure Hierarchy

```
MSS (Market Structure Shift)
    ↓ Strongest — Trend reversal confirmed
    
ChoCH (Change of Character)
    ↓ Moderate — Warning, trend weakening
    
BOS (Break of Structure)
    ↓ Standard — Trend continuation
```

## Multi-Timeframe Structure

**High Probability Setup:**
1. **HTF (4H/Daily):** BOS in progress = with-trend bias
2. **MTF (1H):** ChoCH forming = caution, possible pullback
3. **LTF (15m):** BOS in HTF direction = entry timing

**Or reversal setup:**
1. **HTF:** MSS just formed
2. **MTF:** First ChoCH in new direction
3. **LTF:** BOS confirming new trend

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Why It Fails | Fix |
|---------|--------------|-----|
| Trading every ChoCH as reversal | ChoCHs often just pullbacks | Wait for MSS |
| Ignoring ChoCH warning | Miss trend change early | Reduce size, tighten stops |
| BOS in chop | False breakouts galore | Require HTF trend alignment |
| Confusing internal/external structure | Wrong analysis, wrong trade | Mark swing points clearly |
| Not waiting for close | Wicks fake out | Close beyond = valid |

---

## Quick Structure Checklist

**Before Entry:**
1. Mark swing highs and lows on chart
2. Identify current trend direction (higher highs/lows?)
3. Locate the origin (swing that started trend)
4. Check for MSS (trend change?), ChoCH (warning?), or BOS (continuation?)
5. Align across 2+ timeframes
6. Wait for sweep of liquidity before entry
7. Target opposite liquidity pool

**Exit Signals:**
- MSS against your position = **exit immediately**
- ChoCH against you = tighten stop, consider partial exit
- BOS against you = you've been early/wrong, reassess

## Structure in Ranges vs. Trends

| Environment | Structure Behavior | Strategy |
|-------------|-------------------|----------|
| Strong trend | Clean BOS, rare ChoCH/MSS | With-trend only |
| Weak trend | Frequent ChoCHs | Smaller size, tighter stops |
| Range | No MSS, false BOS | Fade structure at extremes |
| Transition | MSS, then new trend | Enter on retest of origin |

## One-Line Wisdom

> "BOS tells you the trend is healthy. ChoCH whispers it's getting tired. MSS screams it's over. Listen to the hierarchy, don't fight the shift."
