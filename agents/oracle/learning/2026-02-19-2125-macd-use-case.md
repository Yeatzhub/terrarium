## What MACD Measures

**Formula:**
- MACD Line = 12 EMA − 26 EMA
- Signal Line = 9 EMA of MACD Line
- Histogram = MACD Line − Signal Line

**Translation:** Momentum by comparing fast vs. slow moving averages. Shows trend strength and potential changes.

---

## Core Signals

### 1. Crossovers — Primary Entry Signal

**Bullish Crossover (Golden Cross):**
- MACD line crosses **above** signal line
- Confirms upward momentum shift
- Stronger if below zero line (oversold bounce)

**Bearish Crossover (Death Cross):**
- MACD crosses **below** signal line
- Confirms downward momentum shift
- Stronger if above zero line (overbought drop)

**Quality Tiers:**

| Condition | Strength | Action |
|-----------|----------|--------|
| Cross above zero | Strong bullish | Full size entry |
| Cross below zero, then bullish cross | Bullish continuation | Add or enter |
| Cross below zero | Strong bearish | Exit longs, consider short |
| Cross above zero, then bearish cross | Bearish continuation | Exit or hedge |
| Cross near zero, histogram small | Weak signal | Wait or reduce size |

---

### 2. Zero Line Cross — Trend Direction

**MACD Above Zero Line:**
- Bullish trend regime
- Prefer longs
- Bearish crossovers = pullbacks, not reversals

**MACD Below Zero Line:**
- Bearish trend regime
- Prefer shorts
- Bullish crossovers = rallies, not trend changes

**Zero Line Cross:**
- MACD crossing zero = trend change confirmation
- **Bullish:** MACD below zero → crosses up = momentum shift
- **Bearish:** MACD above zero → crosses down = momentum shift

---

### 3. Histogram Divergence — Warning Signal

**Bullish Divergence (Price ↓, Histogram ↑):**
- Price making lower lows
- MACD histogram making higher lows
- **Warning:** Selling exhaustion, potential reversal

**Bearish Divergence (Price ↑, Histogram ↓):**
- Price making higher highs
- MACD histogram making lower highs
- **Warning:** Buying exhaustion, potential reversal

**Critical:** Divergence = warning, not entry signal. Wait for:
- Crossover confirmation, OR
- Break of structure (BOS), OR
- Entry at SMC liquidity sweep

---

### 4. Histogram Analysis — Momentum Strength

| Histogram Pattern | Meaning |
|-------------------|---------|
| Growing positive | Bullish momentum accelerating |
| Shrinking positive | Bullish momentum slowing |
| Growing negative | Bearish momentum accelerating |
| Shrinking negative | Bearish momentum slowing |
| Crossing zero | Momentum shift |

**Strategy:** Enter on first positive histogram bar after series of negative bars (vice versa for shorts).

## MACD + Price Action Confluence

### High Probability Setup: MACD + Structure

**Bullish BOS + MACD Bullish Cross:**
1. Price breaks structure (higher high)
2. MACD bullish crossover below or near zero
3. Entry on pullback to structure or OB
4. Stop below structure
5. Target: Next resistance or 2:1

**Bearish MSS + MACD Bearish Cross:**
1. Price shifts market structure (breaks origin)
2. MACD bearish crossover above or near zero
3. Entry on retest of broken structure
4. Stop above structure
5. Target: Next support or 2:1

### MACD + Trend Alignment

**With-Trend Entries:**
- HTF trend up + MACD bullish cross below zero = strong buy
- HTF trend down + MACD bearish cross above zero = strong sell

**Counter-Trend (Lower Probability):**
- HTF trend down + MACD bullish cross above zero = scalp only
- HTF trend up + MACD bearish cross below zero = scalp only
- Reduce size 50%

## MACD for Trend Filtering

### Long-Only Filter
Only take longs when:
- MACD above zero line, OR
- MACD bullish crossover occurred in last 5 bars

### Short-Only Filter
Only take shorts when:
- MACD below zero line, OR
- MACD bearish crossover occurred in last 5 bars

**Result:** Eliminates 30%+ of false signals by trading with momentum regime.

## MACD Limitations & Fixes

| Limitation | Why It Happens | Fix |
|------------|----------------|-----|
| Lagging signal | Based on moving averages | Pair with price action or RSI |
| False crosses in chop | No trend, oscillating | Require zero line context |
| Late entries | Delayed confirmation | Use histogram early signals |
| Whipsaws | Fast reversals | Require structure confirmation |
| Divergence without reversal | Can persist | Wait for crossover confirmation |

---

## Practical MACD Framework

### Step-by-Step Entry

1. **Check HTF MACD** (Daily/4H) — confirm trend direction
2. **Mark zero line** — above = bullish regime, below = bearish
3. **Wait for cross** — signal line cross in HTF direction
4. **Confirm histogram** — growing in trade direction
5. **Check structure** — BOS or liquidity sweep aligning?
6. **Set entry** — at structure retest or OB/FVG
7. **Set stop** — below/above structure or 1.5× ATR
8. **Target** — 2:1 minimum, trail if trend strong

### Exit Signals

- MACD bearish cross (for longs) = take partial profits
- Histogram shrinks 3+ bars = momentum fading, tighten stop
- MACD approaches zero = trend weakening, consider full exit
- Opposite structure forms (MSS) = exit immediately
