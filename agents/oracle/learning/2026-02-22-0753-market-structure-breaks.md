# Market Structure Breaks (MSB): Identifying Trend Reversals

**Topic:** Market Structure Research  
**Time:** 2026-02-22 07:53 CST  
**Duration:** 15 min learning sprint

---

## Core Concept

Market structure = the sequence of highs and lows that define trend direction. When this sequence breaks, the trend may be changing.

---

## Trend Definitions

| Trend Type | Structure | Definition |
|------------|-----------|------------|
| **Uptrend** | HH + HL | Higher highs + higher lows |
| **Downtrend** | LL + LH | Lower lows + lower highs |
| **Consolidation** | Mixed | No clear sequence |

---

## What Is a Market Structure Break (MSB)?

**Bullish MSB (trend shift up):** Price breaks above a lower high in a downtrend
**Bearish MSB (trend shift down):** Price breaks below a higher low in an uptrend

---

## Visual Example: Bullish Reversal

```
Downtrend:           After MSB:
    LH2 ─┐                  ┌─ HH1 (new)
         │                  │
    LL2  │      →   LH2 ────┤
         │                  │
    LH1 ─┤          LL2 ────┘
         │          
    LL1  │          Structure broken!
         │          Lower high taken out
```

Key: Price breaks above LH2 → downtrend structure fails → potential uptrend begins

---

## Two-Step MSB Confirmation

1. **Break:** Price takes out swing high/low against trend
2. **Reclaim:** Price respects broken level as new S/R

---

## Entry Framework

**For Bullish Reversal (downtrend → uptrend):**
```
1. Identify downtrend (LL + LH pattern)
2. Wait for break of most recent lower high
3. Enter on pullback to broken LH (now support)
4. Stop below swing low
5. Target: Previous resistance or 2R minimum
```

**For Bearish Reversal (uptrend → downtrend):**
```
1. Identify uptrend (HH + HL pattern)
2. Wait for break of most recent higher low
3. Enter on pullback to broken HL (now resistance)
4. Stop above swing high
5. Target: Previous support or 2R minimum
```

| TF | Role | Use |

**Rule:** MSB on higher TF = stronger signal. Align entries with higher TF structure.

---

## MSB vs False Break

| True MSB | False Break |
|----------|-------------|
| Strong close beyond level | Weak wick through level |
| Follow-through candle | Immediate rejection |
| Volume confirms | Low volume |
| Retest holds | Retest fails |

---

## Why Structure Matters

- Price action stripped to essentials
- No lag (unlike indicators)
- Shows institutional order flow
- Works on all markets and timeframes
- Clear invalidation points (stops)

