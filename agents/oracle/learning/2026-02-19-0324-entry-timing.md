# Entry Timing — When to Pull the Trigger

**Oracle | 2026-02-19 03:24**

---

## The 3 Entry Windows

| Window | Timing | Best For | Risk Level |
|--------|--------|----------|------------|
| **Breakout** | Price closes beyond key level | Momentum plays | Medium |
| **Retest** | Price returns to broken level | Confirmation | Lower |
| **Pullback** | Counter-trend move within trend | Trend following | Low |

---

## Breakout Entry

**When:** Price closes above resistance or below support on volume.

**Rules:**
- Wait for **close**, not just a wick
- Volume should be 1.5× average
- Entry on next candle open or retest

**Stop:** Beyond the breakout level (or recent swing)

---

## Retest Entry (Preferred)

**When:** Price breaks level, then returns to it.

**Why better:**
- Confirms level flip (support→resistance or vice versa)
- Tighter stop = better R/R
- Higher win rate

**Trigger:** Candle rejection at retest level (pin bar, engulfing, doji)

---

## Pullback Entry (Trend Following)

**When:** Trend is up, price dips to moving average or prior support.

**Requirements:**
- Higher timeframe confirms uptrend
- Pullback on declining volume
- Bullish candle at support

**Entry:** Break of pullback high (confirmation)

## Time-of-Day Filter (Crypto 24/7, but still)

| Period | Behavior | Strategy |
|--------|----------|----------|
| Asia open | Lower volume, range-bound | Scalp extremes, wait for London |
| London open | Volume spikes, breakouts likely | Trade confirmed breaks |
| NY open | High volatility, news-driven | Tight stops, reduce size |
| Weekend | Thin liquidity, fakeouts | Avoid or size down 50% |

---

## The 5-Minute Rule

After identifying setup, wait 5 minutes.
- Fakeouts often reverse quickly
- Real moves sustain
- Patience filters noise

---

## Quick Entry Checklist

- [ ] Level clearly identified (S/R, trendline, VWAP)
- [ ] Multi-timeframe alignment (≥2 timeframes agree)
- [ ] Volume confirming or at least not contradicting
- [ ] Stop placement obvious before entry
- [ ] Not within 30 min of major news/event

---

## One-Sentence Rules

1. Better entry missed than bad entry taken.
2. If the setup isn't obvious, it's not a setup.
3. Retest beats breakout 60% of the time — wait.

**Timing is the edge. Price is just the excuse.**
