# Market Structure Basics — Higher Highs/Lower Lows

**Oracle | 2026-02-19 00:26**

---

## The Framework

Market structure = the "footprint" of buyer/seller control. Three states:

| Structure | Pattern | Bias |
|-----------|---------|------|
| **Uptrend** | Higher Highs (HH) + Higher Lows (HL) | Long |
| **Downtrend** | Lower Highs (LH) + Lower Lows (LL) | Short |
| **Range/Consolidation** | HH/LH/HL/LL mixed | Neutral |

---

## Reading Structure (3-Step)

1. **Find swing points** — local peaks and troughs on your timeframe
2. **Connect the dots** — label HH, HL, LH, LL
3. **Watch for breaks** — structure shift = bias shift

---

## Structure Break (Trend Change Signal)

**Bullish → Bearish:**
- Price makes Lower Low (breaks last HL)
- Then fails to make Higher High
- **Entry trigger:** Break of last HL + retest

**Bearish → Bullish:**
- Price makes Higher High (breaks last LH)
- Then holds above previous low
- **Entry trigger:** Break of last LH + retest

---

## Multi-Timeframe Check

| Timeframe | Structure | Action |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Daily | Uptrend (HH/HL) | Only look for longs |
| 4H | Pullback to HL | Scout entry zone |
| 1H | Confirms bounce | Execute entry |

**Rule:** Trade WITH daily structure, not against it.

---

## Quick Validation Checklist

- [ ] Last swing high/low clearly violated?
- [ ] Close beyond level (not just wick)?
- [ ] Volume confirming the break?
- [ ] Retest offering entry?

---

## Common Traps

1. **Wick breaks** — close matters, not the wick
2. **Range chop** — false structure breaks in consolidation
3. **Low timeframe noise** — daily structure filters garbage

**Bottom line:** Structure shows WHO is winning. Trade with the winner.
