# VWAP: The Institutional Benchmark for Intraday Trading

**Oracle Quick Study**  
*Topic: Indicator Use Case*  
*Date: 2026-02-21 06:25 PM CT*

---

## TL;DR

VWAP (Volume Weighted Average Price) shows where most volume traded—institutional benchmark. Price above VWAP = bullish control. Below = bearish control. Use for directional bias, entry timing, and profit targets. The only indicator institutional desks actually watch.

---

## The Calculation

```
VWAP = Σ(Price × Volume) / Σ(Volume)

Where price = (High + Low + Close) / 3
```

**In plain English:** VWAP is the average price weighted by volume. It shows where the "smart money" accumulated their position. Institutions benchmark performance to VWAP—buying below, selling above.

---

## VWAP Variants

### Session VWAP (Standard)
- Resets at session open (9:30 AM ET for equities)
- Most accurate for intraday directional bias
- **Use:** Day trading, session-based entries

### Rolling VWAP
- Custom lookback period (e.g., 20 periods)
- Doesn't reset per session
- **Use:** 24-hour markets (crypto, forex)

### Anchored VWAP
- User-defined start point (earnings, swing high/low, date)
- Tracks from specific event/level
- **Use:** Post-event analysis, swing trading context

---

## The Three Bands (Standard Deviation)

Most VWAP tools include bands at 1, 2, and 3 standard deviations:

| Band Distance | Typical Usage |
|-------------|---------------|
| VWAP ± 1σ | Mean reversion zone, chop likely |
| VWAP ± 2σ | Take profit targets, extreme zone |
| VWAP ± 3σ | Rarely tested, strong reversal signals |

**80% of price action** occurs between ±1σ bands. Tests of ±2σ = opportunity.

## VWAP Trading Framework

### Use Case 1: Directional Bias

**VWAP Slope:**
- Rising VWAP = accumulation, bullish
- Falling VWAP = distribution, bearish
- Flat VWAP = consolidation, no control

**Position Rules:**
- Above VWAP: Prefer longs, use VWAP as support
- Below VWAP: Prefer shorts, use VWAP as resistance
- Cross VWAP: Context matters—trend continuation or reversal?

### Use Case 2: Mean Reversion (VWAP Bounce)

**Setup:**
1. Strong trend established (price > VWAP or < VWAP for 30+ min)
2. Price pulls back to VWAP (or ±1σ band)
3. Rejection candle forms (doji, hammer, engulfing)
4. Volume confirms (increase on bounce)

**Long Entry:**
- Price in uptrend above VWAP
- Pulls back to VWAP or lower band
- Bullish candle rejects off VWAP
- Stop below VWAP or candle low
- Target: Upper band or next resistance

**Win Rate:** ~65% in trending conditions, ~45% in choppy markets.

### Use Case 3: Breakout/VWAP Reclaim

**Setup:**
1. Price below VWAP in downtrend
2. Consolidates near VWAP but doesn't break
3. Volume surge as price reclaims VWAP
4. Close above VWAP confirms

**Entry:**
- Close above VWAP with volume
- VWAP now acts as dynamic support
- Add to position on retest of VWAP from above

**This is high-conviction long signal.**

### Use Case 4: VWAP Bands as Targets

**Trend Trade Management:**
- Entry: Above VWAP in uptrend
- Scale 1: At +1σ (33% out, move stop to breakeven)
- Scale 2: At +2σ (33% out)
- Final 33%: Trail or hold to close

**Mean Reversion:**
- Entry: At +2σ in uptrend (expect pullback)
- Target: VWAP or +1σ
- Stop: Above +3σ or recent high

## VWAP by Time of Day

| Period | VWAP Behavior | Strategy |
|--------|---------------|----------|
| **First 30 min** | Establishes, often volatile | Watch, don't chase |
| **Morning** | Trend develops, VWAP holds | Trade with VWAP slope |
| **Midday** | Mean reversion likely | Fade ±2σ bands |
| **Afternoon** | Momentum, VWAP breaks | Momentum continuation |
| **Close** | Institutions benchmark to VWAP | Align or reverse |

**Power Hour (3:00-4:00 PM ET):**
- Price often accelerates toward VWAP
- Institutions adjust positions
- VWAP acts as magnet before close

### VWAP + Order Blocks

## The VWAP Rejection Pattern

**Bullish Rejection:**
1. Price above VWAP for extended period
2. Pulls back to VWAP
3. Long wick below, close above
4. Next candle confirms higher

**Entry:** On close above VWAP after rejection
**Stop:** Below rejection wick
**Target:** Recent high or +1σ band

**Bearish Rejection (mirror):**
- Price below VWAP
- Rallies to VWAP
- Rejected (wick above, close below)
- Short entry

## VWAP for Forex/Crypto (Rolling)

Session-less markets need rolling VWAP:

**Rolling VWAP(24):**
- 24-hour lookback
- Resets every hour (overlapping)
- Shows institutional flow in 24/7 markets

**Crypto nuances:**
- VWAP spikes on weekend low volume
- Whale accumulation shows as VWAP "steps"
- Compare to 7-day VWAP for longer context

---

## VWAP Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Trading VWAP alone** — VWAP shows WHERE volume traded, not direction. Combine with price action.

2. **First 15-minute VWAP** — Session VWAP is unreliable in first 15 minutes. Wait for data.

3. **Ignoring slope** — Flat VWAP = no edge. Only trade with directional slope.

4. **Catching the reclaim (early)** — Wait for CLOSE above/below VWAP, not just wick.

5. **Using VWAP in low volume** — Illiquid stocks = VWAP meaningless. Minimum $5M daily volume.

## VWAP Scoring System

Rate your VWAP setups:

| Factor | Points | Condition |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Trend alignment | +2 | Price aligned with VWAP slope for 30+ min |
| Volume | +1 | Above average on VWAP interaction |
| Structure | +1 | At HTF level (daily support/resistance) |
| Time of day | +1 | Morning/PM session (not midday chop) |
| Band distance | +1 | ±1σ or better (not middle zone) |

**Scoring:**
- 5-6: High conviction, full size
- 3-4: Medium, half size
- 0-2: Skip
