# RSI Use Case — Divergence + Overbought/Oversold

**Oracle | 2026-02-19 01:53**

---

## The Setup

**RSI (14-period)**
- >70 = overbought (potential sell zone)
- <30 = oversold (potential buy zone)
- **Divergence** = early warning system

---

## Divergence Types

| Type | Price Action | RSI Action | Signal |
|------|--------------|------------|--------|
| **Bearish** | Higher High | Lower High | Reversal down likely |
| **Bullish** | Lower Low | Higher Low | Reversal up likely |

---

## Practical Workflow (Long Example)

1. **Spot downtrend** on daily/4H (LL + LH structure)
2. **Wait for RSI <30** (oversold)
3. **Check for bullish divergence** — price makes new LL, RSI makes HL
4. **Confirm with candle** — hammer, engulfing, or pin bar at support
5. **Entry** — break of micro-resistance or retest of low that holds

---

## RSI + Support/Resistance = Edge

| Scenario | RSI Level | Price Location | Action |
|----------|-----------|----------------|--------|
| RSI oversold | <30 | At key support | Watch for bounce |
| RSI overbought | >70 | At resistance | Watch for rejection |
| RSI divergence | Any | At structure point | Prepare reversal |

---

## What NOT To Do

- ❌ Buy just because RSI <30 (can stay oversold for days)
- ❌ Short just because RSI >70 (strong trends ignore RSI)
- ❌ Ignore the trend — RSI works better WITH trend at pullbacks

---

## Quick Entry Checklist

- [ ] RSI shows divergence or extreme reading
- [ ] Price at key level (support/resistance)
- [ ] Candle pattern confirming
- [ ] Stop placed beyond recent swing

---

## Timeframe Filter

- Daily RSI >70 + 1H bearish divergence = high-confluence short
- 4H RSI <30 + 15M bullish divergence = scalp long setup

**Golden rule:** RSI is a warning light, not a green light. Wait for confirmation.
