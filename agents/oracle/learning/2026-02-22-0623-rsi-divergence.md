# RSI Divergence: High-Probability Reversal Signals

**Topic:** Indicator Use Case

**Duration:** 15 min learning sprint

---

## What Is Divergence?

Price makes a new extreme, but RSI doesn't confirm = momentum fading.

---

## Two Types

| Type | Price Action | RSI Action | Signal |
|------|--------------|------------|--------|
| **Bullish** | Lower low | Higher low | Potential reversal up |
| **Bearish** | Higher high | Lower high | Potential reversal down |

---

## Setup Checklist

**For Bullish Divergence (long entry):**
1. Price makes lower low vs previous swing low
2. RSI (14) makes higher low at same point
3. RSI was oversold (<30) on first low
4. Wait for RSI to cross above 30 or price break prior swing high

**For Bearish Divergence (short entry):**
1. Price makes higher high vs previous swing high
2. RSI (14) makes lower high at same point
3. RSI was overbought (>70) on first high
4. Wait for RSI to cross below 70 or price break prior swing low

---

## Example: Bullish Divergence

```
Price:  $100 → $95 → $92 (lower low)
RSI:     28  → 35  → 38  (higher low)
                ↑
           Divergence confirmed
```

Entry: Break above $95 (prior swing low)  
Stop: Below $92  
Target: 2R minimum

---

## Why It Works

- RSI measures momentum strength
- Divergence = momentum diverging from price
- Indicates exhaustion before price confirms
- Institutional footprints often visible first in momentum

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

| Mistake | Correct Approach |
|---------|------------------|
| Trading immediately on divergence | Wait for confirmation (break or cross) |
| Ignoring trend context | Favor divergences WITH trend on higher TF |
| Using alone | Combine with S/R, volume, market structure |
| Wrong RSI period | Stick to 14; adjust only with backtesting |

---

## Profitable Combinations

1. **RSI Divergence + Support/Resistance** = strongest
2. **RSI Divergence + Volume spike** = institutional interest
3. **RSI Divergence + Trend (higher TF)** = pullback entries

---

## Quick Reference

```
Bullish Divergence = Price LL + RSI HL → Look for longs
Bearish Divergence = Price HH + RSI LH → Look for shorts
Always wait for confirmation before entry
```

> "Divergence shows the lie in price action—momentum reveals truth before the move."
