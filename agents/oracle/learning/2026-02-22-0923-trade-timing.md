# Trade Timing: When to Enter Matters as Much as What

**Topic:** Trading Concept - Timing  
**Time:** 2026-02-22 09:23 CST  
**Duration:** 15 min learning sprint

---

## Core Truth

A good setup at the wrong time = losing trade. Timing determines fill quality, slippage, and follow-through probability.

---

## Session Timing (Forex/Futures)

| Session | Time (ET) | Best For |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| **Asian** | 7PM–4AM | Ranges, JPY pairs |
| **London Open** | 3AM–5AM | Breakouts, trend starts |
| **London** | 3AM–12PM | All pairs, best liquidity |
| **NY Open** | 8AM–10AM | Volatility, reversals |
| **NY/London Overlap** | 8AM–12PM | Peak volume, moves |
| **NY Afternoon** | 12PM–5PM | Chop fades |

**Rule:** Trade active sessions. Avoid dead zones.

---

## The Two Best Windows

### 1. London Open (3AM–5AM ET)
- Trend direction often established
- Clean breaks from Asian range
- Less noise than NY open

### 2. NY/London Overlap (8AM–12PM ET)
- Maximum liquidity
- Tightest spreads
- Strongest follow-through

---

## Entry Timing: The 3-Step Check

Before clicking, verify:

| Check | Question | Red Flag |
|-------|----------|----------|
| **Session** | Is this pair active now? | Trading EUR/USD at 2AM ET |
| **Catalyst** | Why will price move NOW? | No news, no level, no structure |
| **Confirmation** | Has setup triggered? | Entering on hope, not signal |

---

## Time-Based Rules

### DO:
- Enter breakouts at session open
- Take trend trades during overlap
- Fade moves into session close
- Wait for the first 15 min after open to settle

### DON'T:
- Enter 10 min before major news
- Trade the last 30 min of Friday
- Chase moves after 12PM ET (fades likely)
- Enter during lunch hours (12PM–2PM ET)

---

## News Timing

| Timing | Action |
|--------|--------|
| **Before news** | Flat or reduce size |
| **During news** | Wait (spikes, slippage) |
| **5-15 min after** | Let direction emerge |
| **30+ min after** | Trade the reaction |

---

## Intraday Entry Precision

**Bad:** Market order at any price  
**Better:** Limit order at key level  
**Best:** Limit order + time window + confirmation

```
Example: Long EUR/USD
- Setup: Bullish MSB on 4H
- Level: 1.0850 support
- Timing: London open (3AM ET)
- Entry: Limit at 1.0850 + 15m candle close confirmation
```

## Timing Scorecard

Rate each trade 0-2 on timing factors:

| Factor | 0 pts | 1 pt | 2 pts |
|--------|-------|------|-------|
| Session | Dead zone | Mid-session | Open/overlap |
| News | 5 min before | No news | Cleared catalyst |
| Setup | Premature | Valid | Confirmed |

**Score 5-6** = High-probability timing  
**Score 3-4** = Acceptable, reduce size  
**Score 0-2** = Skip trade
