## 📏 Box Sizing Guide

| Item Size | Box Type | Where to Get |
|-----------|----------|--------------|
| < 1 lb, flat | Padded envelope | Amazon, Walmart |
| < 1 lb, small | Poly mailer or small box | Dollar store, Amazon |
| 1-5 lbs | USPS Priority boxes (free!) | Order at USPS.com |
| 5-10 lbs | Medium boxes | Home Depot, Lowe's, U-Haul |
| > 10 lbs | Heavy-duty boxes | Home Depot, U-Haul |

### Free USPS Boxes
**Order for free delivery:**
- USPS Priority Mail boxes (various sizes)
- USPS Flat Rate boxes (if shipping long distance)
- Order at: https://store.usps.com/store/results/free-shipping-supplies/_/N-alnxv

**Tip:** Stock up on Priority boxes — they're free and shipping is included in rate.

## 🛡️ Packing Fragile Items

### Electronics/Consoles
1. Wrap item in bubble wrap (2 layers)
2. Place in box with 2" clearance
3. Fill gaps with packing peanuts or crumpled paper
4. Bubble wrap accessories separately
5. Mark box "FRAGILE"

### Collector Cards
1. Penny sleeve → Top loader → Team bag
2. Sandwich between cardboard
3. Padded envelope or small box
4. "Do Not Bend" on envelope

### Glass/Ceramics
1. Wrap individually in bubble wrap
2. Use dividers between items
3. Double-box if valuable
4. Mark "FRAGILE — HANDLE WITH CARE"

## ⚖️ Weighing Items

### Kitchen Scale (< 5 lbs)
- Place box on scale with all contents
- Round up to nearest ounce
- Add 1-2 oz for packing materials if not included

### Bathroom Scale (> 5 lbs)
- Weigh yourself holding box
- Weigh yourself without box
- Subtract = package weight

**eBay's Shipping Calculator:**
- Use exact weight
- Measure box dimensions
- Let eBay calculate based on buyer's location

## 📬 USPS Shipping Methods

| Method | Delivery | Best For |
|--------|----------|----------|
| First Class | 1-5 days | Items < 16 oz, cheap, not urgent |
| Priority Mail | 1-3 days | Most items, includes tracking + insurance ($100) |
| Priority Flat Rate | 1-3 days | Heavy items shipping far |
| Media Mail | 2-8 days | Books, DVDs, media only (slow but cheap) |

**Recommendation:** Use Priority Mail for most items. Free boxes, fast, reliable, includes insurance.

## 🔄 Shipping Workflow

### When Item Sells:
1. **Get notification** (eBay app/email)
2. **Confirm payment** received
3. **Print label** (eBay discount automatically applied)
4. **Pack item** (use checklist above)
5. **Attach label** (tape securely, cover barcode with clear tape)
6. **Drop off** or schedule pickup

### Drop-off Options:
- USPS drop box (small items with postage)
- USPS counter (get receipt = tracking proof)
- Scheduled pickup (free on some routes, $ on others)

### Typical Shipping Costs (eBay rates):

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

- **Underestimating weight** → You pay difference
- **Wrong box size** → Dimensional weight charges
- **Insufficient padding** → Broken items, refunds
- **No tracking** → Can't prove delivery
- **Late shipping** → eBay penalizes seller rating

## 📈 Shipping Best Practices

**Ship fast:**
- Same day if paid before 3 PM
- Next day if paid after 3 PM
- Weekend orders: Ship Monday

**Communicate:**
- "Ships within 1 business day" in listing
- Upload tracking immediately
- Message buyer when item ships

**Protect yourself:**
- Always get tracking
- Take photos before shipping
- Require signature for items > $250
- Consider insurance on items > $100
